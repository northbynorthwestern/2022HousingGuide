All-Gender Housing North vs. South Campus ADA Accessibility Hall vs. College
All-gender housing is a residence option in which students can share a room or suite regardless of their gender identity. Ten dorms on campus offer all-gender housing, which is usually a cluster of all-gender designated rooms on a certain wing or floor. Our information lists the all-gender housing options according to Residential Services. In many dorms, students will only be placed in an all-gender wing or suite if they opt-in, so students who do not opt-in will not be assigned to an all-gender wing or suite. For transparency's sake, we want to acknowledge that not all students have had positive experiences with all-gender housing. The Daily Northwestern discusses this issue in more detail.

Residential Services all-gender housing info 		Generally, South Campus is known for humanities and North Campus is known for STEM, mostly because of proximity to classes. North Campus also has more of a party reputation for its proximity to fraternity quad, but South Campus has more of an artsy vibe and is a shorter walk to downtown Evanston, grocery stores and restaurants. If you want to live in a suite, look to the several dorms on North Campus, but beware the walk South to downtown and classes in the winter. We divided the dorms into North and South Campus based on Foster St. Although Residential Services says that several dorms on campus have elevators, not all dorms are ADA accessible. Students have reported issues with accessibility for specific dorms, which we have noted on that dorm's individual page. We do not have complete information on which residences have ADA accessible rooms, but we have noted that information for dorms that we know do have them. We will update the guide if we get more information. Residential colleges have organized programming, with multiple events happening each week at some dorms. Some residential colleges are thematic and can help you find like-minded people on campus, but even non-thematic residential colleges can encourage community through munchies, social activities and events. A residential hall is a normal dorm.
